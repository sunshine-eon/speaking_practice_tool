You mentioned this term "monetary friction" as one of the levers that you've seen success with. Can you just describe what that actually means?

Totally. It refers to things like trial dynamics, trial length, and trial amount. It also means incentives. So, what is in your product? What do people value and need in order to be successful? 

In Jobless's case, that might be things like app store credits or similar incentives. Those are the two forms of monetary friction we talk about, and then, of course, there's the actual price point. But that's the kind of our focus.
