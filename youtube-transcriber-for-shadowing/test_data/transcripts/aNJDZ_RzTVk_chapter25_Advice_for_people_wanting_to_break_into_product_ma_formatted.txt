Typically, the folks that joined product teams are the highest performers outside of product. They either understand the customer really well and can advise product, or they understand the product very well and can serve customers. My advice for folks that want to break into that is to find a role that is adjacent to product, enabling you to have those experiences and prove yourself. For example, product operations is a good one, business operations is a good one, and more consulting, sales engineering, or solution engineering are good options as well.
