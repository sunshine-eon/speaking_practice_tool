If you ever find yourself saying something like, "that's not my job," that's probably a thing you should do. It may not be your job, and it could very well be someone else's responsibility. As a PM, for better or for worse, you are often the emotional center of the team. It's your job to keep people motivated, excited, and bought into the project.
