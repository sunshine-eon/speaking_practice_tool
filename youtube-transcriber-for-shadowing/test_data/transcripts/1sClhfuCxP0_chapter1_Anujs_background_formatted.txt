There are only three reasons why things do not happen the way you want them to happen as a leader. You can look at a person and say either that person can't do, which is a capability issue, or they won't do, which is a motivation or alignment issue, or they were not set up to do, which is really your problem for not setting up the ways of working and organizational design properly. 

As a leader, do you have the right people in terms of capability? If not, is the right answer for us to coach them, mentor them, or move them to another place because maybe their capability is suited elsewhere? If they won't do, why won't they? Are they not aligned to you? Do they not agree with your vision? Do they not have enough time? You need to really go deeper there. Why won't they do?
